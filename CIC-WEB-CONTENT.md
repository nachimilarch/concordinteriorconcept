## Brand Positioning

**Not** merely an "Interior Design Firm" or a "Construction Company."

> **A Design, Build & Development Consultancy** — a company that transforms land, structures, interiors, and environments into meaningful, sustainable, and future-ready spaces.

This places Concord in a higher-value market category.

---

## Website Style Direction

The website should look like a **premium architecture studio**, not a contractor website.

### Design Language
- Minimal Luxury
- Editorial-style layout
- Large full-width photography
- Plenty of white space
- **Black + Ivory + Warm Beige + Forest Green palette**
- Elegant typography
- Cinematic project images
- No excessive icons
- No cluttered service boxes

**Think:** luxury architecture firms, boutique design studios, high-end development consultancies — **not** construction contractors, modular kitchen companies, civil contractors.

### Theme Tokens (implementation)
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Ink Black | `#181815` | Dark sections, display text |
| Ivory | `#F5F0EB` | Page background |
| Warm Beige | `#B9A27E` | Accent, rules, labels |
| Forest Green | `#2C4A3B` | Primary accent, links, buttons |
| Forest Light | `#7FA08C` | Accent on dark backgrounds |

Fonts: **Cormorant Garamond** (display) · **Inter** (body).
